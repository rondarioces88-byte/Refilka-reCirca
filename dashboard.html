refillka

refillka is ReCirca Management Corp's circular-infrastructure platform — replacing single-use sachets with bulk refilling, in partnership with the City Government of Taguig, NutriAsia, and the Plastic Reboot Program (GEF-UNIDO-DENR).

The 1 Billion Challenge — prevent 1,000,000,000 single-use plastics per year from entering Philippine waste streams by 2030.
