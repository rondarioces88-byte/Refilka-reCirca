06First institutional partner

The City Government of Taguig leads by example.

Taguig City Government — refillka's first institutional client

The City Government of Taguig becomes refillka's inaugural institutional client — procuring household and janitorial cleaning, personal care for employee welfare, and food products for city offices, City Hall, public facilities, and city-managed carinderias. A working demonstration that a managed refill program scales across an entire institution.

09ESG & EPR reporting

Impact data you can report on.

ESG- and EPR-ready impact reporting

Institutional clients receive impact reporting built for ESG and EPR disclosure — sachets avoided, plastic diverted, and CO₂e reduced. It supports compliance with the Extended Producer Responsibility Act (R.A. 11898), which mandates that brand owners take responsibility for their plastic footprint — turning your switch into auditable, reportable progress.

refillka is ReCirca Management Corp's circular-infrastructure platform — replacing single-use sachets with bulk refilling, in partnership with the City Government of Taguig, NutriAsia, and the Plastic Reboot Program (GEF-UNIDO-DENR).
