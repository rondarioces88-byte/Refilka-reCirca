02How refillka works

A simple five-step loop.

1

Order your Kit

Get your Sustainable Living Kit with refill-ready containers.

2

Enjoy & use

Use the products at home — the brands you already trust.

3

Refill

Top up at a refilling station instead of buying new sachets.

4

Return empties

Bring back empty containers to keep them in the loop.

5

Repeat

Save cost, save the planet — again and again.

Every refill earns Circa Points

Each time you refill and return, your Circa ID records the impact and adds points — building toward rewards while keeping plastic out of Taguig's waste streams.

07Founding Green Citizens

How Phase 1 onboarding begins.

Phase 1 is invitation-only

Onboarding opens to invited Green Citizens. The Mayor invites the core LGU — the city council and department heads — targeting roughly 300 people from the city plus around 200 guests and media. From there, the rollout prioritizes stores, then the wider community. There is no consumer app in Phase 1 — participation is QR- and card-based.

Founding onboarding sequence per the refillka product Q&A (PRD).

refillka

refillka is ReCirca Management Corp's circular-infrastructure platform — replacing single-use sachets with bulk refilling, in partnership with the City Government of Taguig, NutriAsia, and the Plastic Reboot Program (GEF-UNIDO-DENR).

The 1 Billion Challenge — prevent 1,000,000,000 single-use plastics per year from entering Philippine waste streams by 2030.
